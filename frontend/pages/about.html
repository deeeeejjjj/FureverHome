What We Stand For

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To build a simple, accessible, and compassionate digital platform that connects animals in need with loving families across Marinduque Province — one adoption at a time.

Our Vision

A Marinduque where every dog and cat has a safe, loving forever home — and where technology bridges the gap between shelters, rescuers, and compassionate adopters.

Our Commitment

We are committed to animal welfare, responsible ownership, and community engagement. Every feature we build is designed with the animals — and the people who love them — at the center.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Everything we do at Furever Home is guided by these principles — from how we designed the platform to how we partner with shelters and treat every adopter.

2025
Project Conceptualized
Furever Home started as a capstone project idea — addressing the lack of digital adoption platforms in Marinduque.
2025
Development Begins
Our 4-person team began designing and building the platform using ASP.NET Core, Bootstrap, and MySQL.
2026
Platform Launched
Furever Home officially launched, partnering with shelters across all 6 municipalities of Marinduque.
Compassion First
Every decision we make prioritizes the well-being of the animals and the experience of the adopters. No animal is just a listing.
Transparency
We show honest pet profiles, clear adoption statuses, and open communication between all parties — no surprises, no hidden processes.
Community-Centered
We were built by Marinduque students, for the Marinduque community — local context and local needs shaped everything we built.
Responsible Adoption
We screen applicants, provide care resources, and follow up post-adoption to ensure pets go to safe, prepared homes.
Innovation with Purpose
We use technology not for its own sake, but to solve real problems — making adoption easier, faster, and more accessible for everyone.
